Role Playing Games (RPG)

Role Playing Games are better known by it's vertical look and horizontal camera movement. RPG can be defined as virtual Dungeons & Dragons tributes.

History

RPG started in the late 60' in Boston College as a "Simulated Society" (or "SimSoc" for short) by it's creator William A. Gamson. It was a Simulation game used in Universities and other groups to teach about sociology, politic sience and effective communication.
After it's appear, in 1974 Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) was published, using the same mechanics as "WarGames" introducing Fantasy. This game is still played between groups of people who likes Fantasy
This 2 grandfathers of the commonly known RPG gave the start of what would become one of the most played game generes around the world. This leads to Japan, where are the producers of the biggest amount of RPG games in the world, known by JRPG (Japan Role Playing Games)
